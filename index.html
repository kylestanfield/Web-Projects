    A few facts about Stallman:

  • Wrote a variety of widely used programs (make, gcc, gdb, emacs among others) as a part of his open source operating system, the GNU project
  • Founded the Free Software Foundation, or FSF, which promotes user's rights to study, distribute, and modify their computer software
  • Does not own a cellular phone out of principle
  • Refuses to own a tie, as it symbolizes submission to your employer
